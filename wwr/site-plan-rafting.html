Overview

Purpose

The purpose for a rafting company would be to provide customers with detailed information about the rafting services offered by the company, as well as to help customers plan and book their rafting adventures in an easy and convenient way.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

People interested in outdoor activities, extreme sports, and river adventures. They can be young adults, active families, tourists, and corporate groups.

What do they want?

An exciting and challenging experience in a natural environment.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

Detailed information about rafting services, including different types of excursions, rapid difficulty levels, excursion schedules, and prices.
